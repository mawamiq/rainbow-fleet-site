About the Hall

A warm, spacious venue for every celebration.

Located on Main Old Airport Road in Fazal Town Phase II, Rawalpindi, Mir Jaan Shadi Hall has hosted thousands of weddings, walimas, mehndis and family gatherings. We pride ourselves on a welcoming atmosphere, attentive staff and affordable pricing that lets you focus on the moment — not the bill.
